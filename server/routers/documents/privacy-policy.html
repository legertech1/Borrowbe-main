For Business Users:

Name, email, Business Name and location are the mandatory fields for business users. Nickname, business address, business phone number, Business Logo, YouTube link of item, social media link, item location on Google Maps, Geo Location from Google, Profile Picture are optional for the business users.

For Individual Users:

Name, email and location are the mandatory fields for individual users. Nickname, phone number, YouTube link of item, social media link, item location on Google Maps, Profile Picture are optional for the individual users

After registering, a User's profile is established. This profile will contain certain User data, like your name, ID, location, and public profile picture. You'll need this information in order to use the BorrowBe app and create a profile. Your listing will be visible to other members of the BorrowBe community and will show where you use the service. Information about your BorrowBe profile can also be read by Users who are not members of BorrowBe. Search engines may gather information about you, your usage, and your BorrowBe profile.

We might grant the Employers access to your personal information when providing you with our services. Payment processors, AWS servers, and MongoDB Database are some of our third-party service provider partners.

The personal data necessary to enable the utilization of features or functionality that establish a connection between the Services and a third-party platform or social media network may be shared with said platform or network. This can be achieved through actions such as logging into the Services using your third-party account, or establishing a linkage between your Services account and a third-party service. The control over the utilization of personal data by a third party is beyond our purview.

At BorrowBe, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to transparent data collection practices. When you log in using Google or Facebook, we only collect your name, email address, and profile picture. Rest assured, your email address is securely stored and will not be visible to other users.

The name and profile image retrieved from Facebook or Google will be set as your default display information. You can edit this information as desired within your profile settings. The only information visible to others is the name and profile picture you choose to display. If you log in with a Google or Facebook account, your profile image and name will be displayed.
